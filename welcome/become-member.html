How to become member?

  • Fill out the registration form and put it in the "new members" box on te ground floor.
  • Each TechTue meeting the applications are read out of the box.
  • One month passes, during which you need 2 sponsors (members that trust you around) to sign your form.
  • We send you an email with further instructions on how to pay your membership fee.
